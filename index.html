what is pho?

Pho is a popular street food in Vietnam. The dish consists of a soup broth, linguine shaped rice noodles, herbs and a variety of choice meats. The dish is traditionally eaten for breakfast or lunch, but can be eaten at any time of the day. It is very common to find different widths of noodles used when cooking pho where each restaurant creates it according to local customs.
